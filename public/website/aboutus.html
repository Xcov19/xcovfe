Project XCoV19 aims to build a patient management software that auto-refers patients to the nearest hospital, streamlines patient flow and models the spread of the virus in order to better anticipate hospital needs.

Current hospital management systems (HMS) are not designed for such emergencies. So we use a novel approach because we leverage a model that is flexible, low cost, and requires minimal training time.
